About Our Company

Innovation, Quality, and Sustainability at Our Core

Weaving Excellence Since Our Foundation

PT. Rindang Jati Spinning has been a leader in the textile industry, specializing in premium yarn production and high-quality fabric manufacturing. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize textile production through innovation and quality.

Today, we stand as a testament to Indonesian craftsmanship, combining traditional expertise with modern technology to deliver products that meet global standards.
